### Multiline cursor operations

##### 1. Multiline add: 
  * Press **CRTL** + **V**
  * Select lines (up and down keys)
  * Press **SHIFT** + **I**
  * Write sth
  * Press **ESC**

##### 2. Multiline remove:
  * Press **CRTL** + **V**
  * Select lines (up, down, left and right keys)
  * Press **DELETE**
